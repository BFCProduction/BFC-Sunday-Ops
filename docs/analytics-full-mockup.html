Across 38 Sundays since 2021 where temperature at service time was below 32°F, average in-person attendance was 1,498 — about 114 fewer people than the all-Sunday average of 1,612.

Church Online unique viewers on those same Sundays averaged 342, vs. the overall average of 282 — suggesting about 60 additional online viewers on freezing Sundays. The drop is partly a shift, not a pure loss.

The effect is stronger for 9am (−9.2%) than 11am (−4.8%), likely because 9am attendees face the coldest part of the morning.
